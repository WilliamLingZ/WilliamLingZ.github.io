Lakes

Many lakes in the Kananaskis Region are large, deep and nested between mountains. In addition they are home to several species of fish which typically feed at dawn or dusk. As a result Kananaskis Country lakes can make good locations for fishing trips as during the early morning and late afternoon catches are frequent. The time in between can be spent hiking or admiring scenery.
