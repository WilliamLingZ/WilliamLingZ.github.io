Meadows

Between the peaks of the Kananaskis mountains lies a plethora of meadows. These vast stretches of flat land typically contain rivers and large quantities of small critters. It is not uncommon to hear chatter between the wildlife from dawn until dusk. Many insects live around these meadows so if you are planning on exploring them, make sure to bring bug spray!
