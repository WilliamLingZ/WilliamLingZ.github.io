Mountains

The mountains of Kananaskis Country are very close to each other and many of them chain together to make large ranges of high altitude terrain. As a result climbing to their peaks can result in very rewarding views. Many different forms of wildlife live within the valleys and peaks of these mountains, including bears, squirrels, deer, and various birds.
